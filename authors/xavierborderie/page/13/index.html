    Everything you need to know about PrestaShop 1.7.0.0

    ...but were afraid to ask -- a.k.a. the 1.7 FAQ

This version is a great step forward for PrestaShop, both for developers with the inclusion of the Symfony framework and the revamping of the theme system, and for merchants with a focus on easing the first sale.

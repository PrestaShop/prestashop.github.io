This November, the PrestaShop and Sylius communities will meet in **Lyon, France** for the first-ever **PS Summit powered by PrestaShop & Sylius**: a two-day conference designed to bring together the open source ecommerce community and the wider ecosystem.

## Save the date

- When: **November 4–5, 2026**
- Where: **Palais des Congrès in Lyon, France**
- Format: Conference with hands-on workshops, dedicated tracks, and technical sessions
- Audience: 500+ professionals from the Sylius and PrestaShop ecosystems (developers, agencies, partners, merchants, and more).

![Picture PS Summit 2026](/assets/images/2026/05/PS_Summit_Buildblog.png)

## Take the stage: we’re looking for speakers

We’d love to see members of our open source community on stage.

We’re inviting developers, merchants, agency professionals, maintainers, contributors, and ecosystem experts to share:

- experiences and lessons learned
- ideas worth exploring
- projects worth sharing
- practical takeaways for the community.

### Suggested topics

Topics can include:

- Open source and community initiatives
- AI and commerce
- Technical deep-dives and architecture
- Merchant success stories
- Case studies and live demos
- Business and ecosystem insights

First-time speakers are absolutely welcome, alongside experienced conference speakers.

## What speakers receive

✅ Conference pass  
✅ Invitation to the opening dinner  
✅ Speaker starter pack  
✅ Travel and accommodation support  

## Languages

The main conference language is **English**, and we also want to accept a few presentations in **French**.

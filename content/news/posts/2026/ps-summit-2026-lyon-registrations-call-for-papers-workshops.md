We are excited to announce that the first-ever **PS Summit powered by PrestaShop & Sylius** is officially taking shape! This November, our communities will meet in Lyon, France, to bring together the open source ecommerce community and the wider ecosystem. Registrations are now open, our Call for Papers is ready for your proposals, and we have an exclusive cross-community workshop planned just before the main event. 

## Secure your spot

![Picture PS Summit 2026](/assets/images/2026/05/PS_Summit_Buildblog.png)

Registrations for PS Summit are now open and tickets are limited! On November 5, join over 500 developers, contributors, partners, service providers, merchants, and industry professionals from both ecosystems for a full day of learning, networking, and collaboration. 

The day will feature an opening keynote by PrestaShop & Sylius CEO Mikołaj Król, who will reveal what's on the horizon for both platforms, including an exclusive sneak peek at PrestaShop 10 and Sylius 3. 

Be sure to [register for the main event here](https://www.ps-summit.com/en/content/ps-summit-conferences-exposition).

## Level up with our workshop

What happens when you put PrestaShop developers in a Sylius workshop, and vice versa? We'll find out on **Wednesday, November 4th**! Join us for a morning of hands-on sessions where you can learn the foundations of Sylius with Academy Creator and Key Contributor **Gracjan Józefczyk**, or dive into PrestaShop with Tech Evangelist and Core Maintainer **Krystian Podemski**. 

![Picture PS Summit Workshop 2026](/assets/images/2026/07/WorkshopPSSummit26.jpeg)

In the afternoon, we'll bring everyone together for a cross-framework hackathon to put your newly acquired knowledge to the test and build something real. Tickets are free but very limited, so don't miss out. You can [register for the workshop here](https://www.ps-summit.com/en/content/workshop-and-hackathon-day). If you also wish to attend the Conference & Exhibition Day on November 5, you will need to register separately for the event.

## Take the stage: we’re looking for speakers

We’d love to see members of our open source community on stage. We invite developers, merchants, agency professionals, maintainers, contributors, and ecosystem experts to share their experiences, practical takeaways, and projects. First-time and experienced speakers are all welcome!

**Suggested topics include:**  
🌍 Open source and community initiatives  
🤖 AI and commerce  
⚙️ Technical deep-dives and architecture  
🏆 Merchant success stories  
📊 Case studies and live demos  
💼 Business and ecosystem insights  

**What speakers receive:**  
Selected speakers get a **conference pass**, an **invitation to our opening dinner**, a **speaker starter pack**, and **travel and accommodation support**.

## Submit your proposal

The main conference language is **English**, and we also want to accept a few presentations in **French**.

If you’ve ever wanted to share your experience with the community, this is a great opportunity to do it and to be part of the first edition of PS Summit in Lyon.

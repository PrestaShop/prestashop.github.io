On March 31st, 2026, we hosted the first PrestaShop Project Live Update of 2026. This was a special edition: [**Mikołaj Król**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bitbager/) (CEO), [**Damian Murawski**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/damian-murawski/) (COO), and [**Olivier Binet**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olivierbinet/) (General Manager) joined us for the first time together since PrestaShop's acquisition. The trio said hi, answered community questions, and shared their perspective on the project's direction. We also covered the latest releases and gave an in-depth look at the upcoming One Page Checkout feature.

PrestaShop Public OSS Demo - March 2021 session

We held the third session of the open source project's public demo

Written by
Mathieu Ferment
Tech Manager
Published

Apr 9, 2021

Share this

The project’s third monthly public demo session was held and streamed live on March 31, 2021.

The session’s agenda was as follows:

  • 00:00 Waiting for everybody to join
  • 01:30 Beginning of the session, introduction
  • 02:05 Presentation of the agenda
  • 03:18 Event rules
  • 04:33 Project announcements
  • 05:12 First demonstration - 1.7.8 Feature Flags by @matks
  • 10:00 Second demonstration - Backlog cleaning by @hibatallahAouadni
  • 24:15 Questions & Answers
  • 01:02:30 Closure

You can use the timestamps to jump directly to the parts you want to watch.

Watch the replay on YouTube

Next session in April

The next session will be streamed live on Wednesday, April 28, 2021 at 2pm CEST.

We expect this session to be particularly interesting as PrestaShop 1.7.8 should have entered Feature Freeze by this time, meaning many projects will be complete.

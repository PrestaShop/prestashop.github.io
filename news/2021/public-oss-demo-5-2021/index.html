PrestaShop Public OSS Demo - May 2021 session

We held the fifth session of the open source project's public demo

Written by
Mathieu Ferment
Tech Manager
Published

May 26, 2021

Share this

The project’s fifth monthly public demo session was held and streamed live on May 26, 2021.

The session’s agenda was as follows:

  • 00:00 Waiting for the stream to begin
  • 00:46 Beginning of the session, introduction
  • 00:59 Presentation of the agenda
  • 02:05 Event rules
  • 03:20 Project announcements
  • 04:12 Demonstration #1 - Experimental Product Page in 1.7.8 by @MatShir and @jolelievre
  • 33:50 Demonstration #2 - How to declare your module’s multistore compatibility by @matthieu-rolland
  • 38:46 Demonstration #3 - Linklist module v5.0.0: introducing multistore compatibility by @kpodemski
  • 46:20 Demonstration #4 - Menu translation improvements by @eternoendless
  • 59:10 Questions & Answers
  • 01:00:24 Closure

You can use the timestamps to jump directly to the parts you want to watch.

Next session in June

The next session will be streamed live on Wednesday, June 30, 2021 at 2pm CEST.

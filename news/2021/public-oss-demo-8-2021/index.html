PrestaShop Public OSS Demo - August 2021 session

We held the eighth session of the open source project's public demo

Written by
PrestaShop team
Published

Sep 2, 2021

Share this

The project’s eighth monthly public demo session was held and streamed live on September 1, 2021.

The session’s agenda was as follows:

  • 00:00 Beginning of the session, introduction
  • 02:21 PHP8 compatibility (@matks and @atomiix)
  • 09:38 Improving PrestaShop templating (@Hlavtox)
  • 23:00 Improve our JS codebase using static types with TypeScript (@NeOMakinG)
  • 35:10 Documentation for 8.0 (@matks)
  • 43:36 Symfony form demo module (@JevgenijVisockij)

You can use the timestamps to jump directly to the parts you want to watch.

Watch the replay on YouTube

Next session in October

The next session will be streamed live on Wednesday, October 6, 2021 at 4pm CEST. See you then!

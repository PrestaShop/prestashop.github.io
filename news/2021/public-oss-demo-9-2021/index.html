PrestaShop Public OSS Demo - September 2021 session

We held the ninth session of the open source project's public demo

Written by
PrestaShop team
Published

Oct 7, 2021

Share this

The project’s ninth monthly public demo session was held and streamed live on October 6, 2021.

The session’s agenda was as follows:

  • 00:00 Beginning of the session, introduction
  • 02:35 Next PrestaShop version by @eternoendless
  • 32:38 Product page discovery by @MatShir and @TristanLDD
  • 47:20 Content style guide by @Julievrz and @prestascott
  • 01:01:29 Questions & Answers

You can use the timestamps to jump directly to the parts you want to watch.

Watch replay on YouTube

Also, you can take a look at the slides presented during the demo here.

As mentioned by @eternoendless, our plan for the next versions of PrestaShop is a proposal. We are looking for feedback and ideas about it, so please join us on Slack to let us know what you think!

Next session in November

Save the date: the next demo will be streamed live on Wednesday, November 3, 2021, at 4 pm CEST. See you then!

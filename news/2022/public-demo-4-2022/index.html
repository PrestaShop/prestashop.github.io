PrestaShop Project Public Demo - June 2022 session

We held the fourth session of this year open source project's public demo

Written by
PrestaShop team
Published

Jun 2, 2022

Share this

The project’s fourth session of this year edition of our public demo was held and streamed live on June 1st, 2022.

The session’s agenda was as follows:

  • 00:00 Beginning of the session, introduction, project news
  • 06:35 Update on the Horizontal Migration by @matks and @tegbessou
  • 21:13 PHP 8.1 compatibility by @atomiix
  • 29:25 Improvements for Email alerts module by @marsaldev
  • 34:21 Questions & Answers + Overview of the community Pull Requests

You can use the timestamps to jump directly to the parts you want to watch.

Watch replay on YouTube

Also, you can take a look at the slides presented during the demo here.

We are available for feedback about the project and the software. We invite everyone to join us on Slack.

Next session in July

Save the date: the next demo will be streamed live on Wednesday, July 6th, 2022, at 4 PM CET. See you then!

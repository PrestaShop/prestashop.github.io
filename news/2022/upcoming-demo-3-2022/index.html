Announcing April PrestaShop Public OSS Demo

Meet us for the third session of the open source project's public demo in 2022

Written by
PrestaShop team
Published

Mar 31, 2022

Share this

Friendly reminder, the next session of the public PrestaShop demo from the maintainers team will happen Wednesday, April 6, 2022 at 4pm CEST!

We expect to share with you project news and present 3 topics:

  • New distribution API @atomiix
  • Front office improvements by @Hlavtox
  • Current hummingbird theme state by @NeOMakinG

Join us on YouTube!

The session will be kept in record on YouTube, so that even if you can’t attend to the live stream, you will still be able to catch up later.

See you then!

PrestaShop Live Update - January 2023

The project's first live update was held for the first time this year

Written by
Krystian Podemski
Tech Evangelist @ PrestaShop
Published

Jan 12, 2023

Share this

The project’s first live update was held for the first time this year and streamed live on January 11th, 2023.

The session’s agenda was as follows:

  • 00:00 Beginning of the session: the introduction
  • 02:50 Project news: PrestaShop 8.0.1 is available
  • 03:26 Project news: Autoupgrade 4.15 is available
  • 07:01 Demo: Hummingbird theme, news & walkthrough, by @NeOMaking
  • 30:05 Demo: WebP and Avif in PrestaShop: multiple image format generation, by @matthieu-rolland
  • 38:16 Questions & answers (do you have any questions? Fill this form and we’ll answer in the next session).

You can use the timestamps to jump directly to the parts you want to watch.

Watch replay on YouTube

Below are links to projects mentioned during this live update:

  • PrestaShop 8.0.1
  • Autoupgrade 4.15
  • Hummingbird
  • Multiple image format generation

Also, you can take a look at the slides presented during the demo here.

We are available for feedback about the project and the software. We also invite everyone to join us on Slack.

Next live update in February

Save the date: the next live update will be streamed live on Wednesday, February 8th, 2023 at 4 pm (Paris time) on YouTube. See you then!

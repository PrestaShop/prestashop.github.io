PrestaShop Live Update - September 2023

A summary of what happened during the September 2023 Live Update

Written by
Krystian Podemski
Tech Evangelist @ PrestaShop
Published

Sep 15, 2023

Share this

The project’s live update was held for the eighth time this year and streamed live on September 13th, 2023.

The session’s agenda was as follows:

  • 00:00 Beginning of the session: the introduction
  • 02:30 Project news: PrestaShop Developer Conference
  • 04:28 Project news: PrestaShop 8.1.1, 8.0.5 and 1.7.8.10
  • 08:56 Demo: changes in the developer documentation by @thomasnares
  • 43:00 Q&A session

You can use the timestamps to jump directly to the parts you want to watch.

Save the date: the next live update will be streamed live on Wednesday, October 11th, 2023 at 4 pm (Paris time) on YouTube. See you then!

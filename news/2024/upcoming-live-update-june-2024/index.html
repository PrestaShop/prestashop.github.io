Announcing PrestaShop Project Live Update June 2024

Let's meet for the second time in 2024

Written by
Krystian Podemski
Tech Evangelist @ PrestaShop
Published

Jun 14, 2024

Share this

The next Live Update for the PrestaShop Project in 2024 will happen on Wednesday, June 19th, 2024 at 4 pm (Paris time) on YouTube!

We will present an overview of the recently released PrestaShop 9 Alpha. This session will cover some of the new features and improvements introduced in this latest version.

Additionally, the Seamless Upgrade & Extensibility team from PrestaShop SA will join us to share their latest projects, with a focus on the recent improvements to the autoupgrade module. This segment will introduce a new team that handles a set of solutions and guidelines designed to facilitate PrestaShop Core, Modules, and Themes upgrades.

Join us on YouTube!

Remember that at the end of every meeting, there is a Q&A session. You can ask your questions during the presentation or beforehand. If you have questions related to the open source project, feel free to let us know in advance using this form. We will do our best to answer them during the Q&A session.

The session will be kept in record on YouTube, so even if you can’t attend the live stream, you will still be able to catch up later.

See you then!

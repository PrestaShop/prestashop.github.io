    PrestaShop Developer Conference 2025 Filmed Sessions - Developer Enablement and Growth

    Watch sessions on Hummingbird 2.0, expert certification, PrestaShop Enterprise and Core contributions

    Written by
    Patrick Coffre
    OS Communication Specialist
    Published

    Jan 28, 2026

    Share this

    The third PrestaShop Developer Conference took place in November 2025. Three weeks ago, we launched a blog series exploring the event’s sessions and sharing key insights with the community. Last week’s focus was artificial intelligence in PrestaShop development. This week, we’re turning our attention to developer enablement and growth.

    If you missed the event or want to revisit the key moments, we’ve made all sessions available on PrestaShop’s YouTube channel.

    From the Hummingbird theme version 2.0 to call for contributions, this week’s sessions cover how we enable and support developers. Let’s dive into the key takeaways from each session—click any image below to watch the full video.

    Hummingbird Theme 2.0: Building More Accessible PrestaShop Stores

    Discover with Alexis Guyomar, fullstack developer and Thibault Blivet, Web integrator, both from PrestaShop, how the brand-new Hummingbird 2.0 theme sets a higher standard for PrestaShop. With over 95% compliance with European Accessibility Act (EAA) standards, Hummingbird 2.0 enables merchants to build online stores that are accessible to all customers. This session covers the theme’s accessibility enhancements, performance optimizations, and developer improvements that establish it as a solid, future-ready foundation for ecommerce projects.

    PrestaShop Expert Certification Core Skills

    During this conference, Marine Petit, Product Manager - Expert Experience and Fabien Moreau, VP Engineering, both from PrestaShop, explore the new PrestaShop Experts certification program, featuring the PrestaShop Core Certification. Learn about the program’s structure, the technical skills it validates, and strategies for highlighting your certification in your business.

    A New Developer Experience with PrestaShop Enterprise

    This presentation is given by Gautier Boudeweel, Senior Engineering Manager, PrestaShop and Soukaïna Barramou, Product Manager PrestaShop Enterprise, PrestaShop. Both speakers showcase PrestaShop Enterprise’s enhanced Developer Experience designed for merchant implementations. Installation is straightforward, while the tooling is robust: CLI tools, CI/CD pipelines, secure environments. Continuous quality validation runs on all customizations pre-deployment, guaranteeing clean, guideline-compliant code.

    Call for Contributions: From Early Successes to Future Challenges

    Do you want to contribute to PrestaShop’s Core but don’t know where to start? This talk, given by Paule Morizot, Head Of Product, PrestaShop and Iliès Bahloul, Product Manager, PrestaShop, breaks down the contribution journey: picking topics, following best practices, working with the Core team, and handling code review. You’ll learn how to create contributions that align with project expectations and maximize your PR success rate.

    The sessions in this post cover the full spectrum of developer growth within the PrestaShop ecosystem. Watch the videos, explore the tools and programs presented, and take the next step in your PrestaShop journey, whether that’s using Hummingbird 2.0, pursuing certification, trying Enterprise, or submitting your first pull request.
